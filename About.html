Lets Explore Our Website

        
 Singaji

      Experience the serene beauty and spiritual ambiance of Singaji, a sacred site
       nestled along thebanks of the Narmada River. Pay homage to the revered deity 
       and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this hallowed sanctuary.
      
     
  Jayanti Mata

      Discover the ancient mystique of Jayanti Mata, a revered temple dedicated to
       the divine goddess. Set amidst picturesque surroundings, 
      this spiritual haven offers solace and serenity to pilgrims and travelers alike.
      
   
Power plant

      Witness the marvels of modern engineering at the Power Plant, where cutting-edge 
      technology converges with natural beauty.Explore the intricacies of electricity 
      generation while admiring the scenic vistas that surround this industrial landmark.
      
   
       
   Omkareshwar

      Journey to the sacred island of Omkareshwar, where spirituality meets natural 
      splendor. Explore ancient temples,  take a boat ride along the tranquil waters 
      of the Narmada River, and immerse yourself in the divine aura of this sacred site.
      
   
    Kishor Smarak

      Pay homage to the legacy of Kishor Smarak, a revered figure whose contributions 
      have  left an indelible mark on the cultural landscape of Knandwa.
       Explore museums, galleries, and monuments dedicated to his memory, celebrating 
           his life and achievements.
      
    
   Tulja_Bhawani

      Experience the fervent devotion and religious fervor at Tulja Bhawani, a revered shrine 
      dedicated to the divine goddess. Bask in the spiritual ambiance, partake in rituals,
       and seek blessings amidst the sacred surroundings.
      
     
   Dadaji Maharaj

      Discover the teachings and wisdom of Dadaji Maharaj, a revered saint whose spiritual legacy 
      continues to inspire generations. Visit ashrams, attend discourses, and immerse yourself 
      in the profound teachings  of this revered spiritual master.
      
     
  Hanumantiya Tapu

      Escape to the tranquil oasis of Hanumantiya Tapu, where nature's beauty unfolds in all
       its glory.Relax on pristine beaches, indulge in water sports, and soak in the 
       breathtaking vistas of this idyllic island getaway.
      
     
  Narmada Sagar Dam

      Marvel at the engineering marvel that is the Narmada Sagar Dam, a monumental structure that
       harnesses the power of the mighty Narmada River. Take a guided tour, learn about its
         significance, and witness the awe-inspiring spectacle of this colossal edifice.
